Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta as per package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix the boiled pasta with the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve.
